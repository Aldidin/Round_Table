About Us

Welcome to Round Table, your ultimate destination for all things board games! At Round Table, we bring together enthusiasts and newcomers alike to explore the captivating world of tabletop gaming. Whether you're into strategy games, classic board games, or the latest in cooperative and competitive play, our club has something for everyone.
Join us at Round Table to experience an inclusive and friendly community where every game night is an adventure. From hosting weekly game sessions and tournaments to providing a vast library of board games, we aim to create a space where fun, creativity, and camaraderie come to life.
Come roll the dice with us and be part of an unforgettable gaming journey!

Games Available!

Dungeons & Dragons

A fantasy tabletop RPG where players create characters and embark on adventures guided by a Dungeon Master. Players navigate through quests, battle monsters, and explore fictional worlds, using dice rolls to determine the outcomes of their actions.

Catan

Catan is a strategic board game where players become settlers building and expanding settlements on the island of Catan. The goal is to be the first to reach 10 victory points by building settlements, cities, and roads, and by achieving special milestones. Players trade resources with each other to gather the necessary materials, making negotiation a crucial part of the game.

The Crew

Is a cooperative trick-taking card game where players work together as a team of astronauts on a mission to explore space. Unlike traditional trick-taking games, The Crew requires players to complete specific tasks, such as winning certain cards or taking tricks in a specific order, all while communicating with each other using limited information.

Carcassonne

A tile-placement game where players build a medieval landscape, scoring points by completing cities, roads, and fields.

Pandemic

A cooperative game where players work together to stop global pandemics by treating infections and finding cures.
